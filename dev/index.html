Supra Cow 2: Day One is a beta of the game Supra Cow 2, a game which a lot has waited for this week.

Supra Cow 2 is a sequel of the game supra cow 1 (you can play it here), in which a cow escapes Black Mesa after The Resonance Cascade (yea, its somehow tied to the lore of Half-life). In supra cow 1, after the successful escape from the facility, the cow escapees from Black Mesa in the hills of New Mexico. But after killing a box, the box god punishes supra cow and teleports it back to Black Mesa, but 10 years before the Resonance Cascade. Now Supra Cow has to save humanity from unforeseen consequences.

The game is developed on Turbowarp, a mod for Scratch 3.0.

The game is pretty technological for it's engine Turbowarp, having 3D sound, a whole physics engine, lighting, and more things you'd like to see in an action game.
